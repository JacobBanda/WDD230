Current Event Section

In a delightful turn of events in Monterrey, the city's renowned Taco Festival has just been named the "World's Most Delicious Celebration" by a prestigious international food magazine! The annual festival, which showcases Monterrey's culinary prowess with an array of mouthwatering taco creations, has taken the global food scene by storm. Food critics from around the world have been singing praises about the exquisite flavors and innovative taco combinations that Monterrey chefs have been serving up. This prestigious recognition is not only a testament to the city's rich gastronomic culture but also a cause for celebration among Monterrey's taco aficionados. So, if you're a fan of tacos, Monterrey is undoubtedly the place to be for an unforgettable culinary adventure!
